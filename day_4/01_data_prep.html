Data used by OGGM#

In this notebook, we want to showcase what OGGM does best: preparing data for your modelling workflow.

We use preprocessed directories which contain most data available from the OGGM shop to illustrate how these could be used to inform data-based workflows.

Preprocessed directories with additional products#

We are going to use the South Glacier example taken from the ITMIX experiment. It is a small (5.6 km2) glacier in Alaska.
